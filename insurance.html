Insurance

  • Insurance

    Our insurance broker Jens Jensen jej@firstbrokers.dk can verify our insurance conditions. Moving best ensures through our quality management that all hauliers have the necessary insurance, as this is an important part of any transport. For compulsory damage done under the Road Act, the statutory motor liability insurance applies. For liability for damage that does not fall under the Road Traffic Act, Flytbedst has taken out a special carrier liability insurance that insures the goods in addition to the rules in force in the CMR Convention. Goods: DKK 400,000 per tonnes load capacity, min. DKK 1,000,000 and max. 3,200,000 kr. transport.

    Antiques, art objects and the like: Max. 40,000 kr. packages

  • WINE, TOBACCO AND SPIRITUS

    Max. cover of DKK 80,000, - per transport, which also covers the customer's own freight insurance. The insurance does not cover damage or loss of precious metals, money, securities, documents, jewels, gems and other valuables.

    Supplementary insurance policies can be taken out.

    Visible damage must be reported immediately. Not visible must be reported within 8 days of transport and covered only if the damage can only be attributed to the transport carried out. Moving best must be informed before transport if there are any goods that are particularly fragile. All insurances only cover if Flytbestst hauliers handle the transported goods alone.

    INSURANCE - INTERNATIONAL TRANSPORT

    International transport is carried out in accordance with the CMR Convention. The amount of compensation is currently. 8.33 sdr / kg. If you have any questions about our insurances, you can call the sales department on telephone 30 30 60 60
